Rotaract Club of Deonar

Rotaract Club of Deonar came into existence on the 16th of June, 1989 under the Presidential leadership of Rtr. Dnyanesh Amonkar when Rtn. Kulbhusha Jetly was the president of Rotary Club of Deonar. A group of 17 young and enthusiastic youngsters started on a journey to thrive for excellence


Year after year the club has maintained its strength and we are proud to have some of our best members leading the club as Presidents. Today we at the Rotaract Club of Deonar, are about 30-40 Rotaractors, from Anushaktinagar, Chembur, Kurla, Vile Parle etc


We have an excellent mix of working professionals ( 91 Springboard, D.B Realty, RUR Greenlife, Accenture, Ernst and Young, Deloitte, L.K.P Securities, Reliance Broadcast Ltd, Teach for India etc ) and budding professionals (from institutes like H.R College, Somaiya, M.G.M College, Vivekanand Education Societyâ€™s Institute of Technology, SIES, K.J Somaiya College of Engineering, IIM Kozhikode, Shah and Anchor Engineering college ). The professional expertise includes Marketing, HR, Medicine, Finance, Law, Software Engineering, Travel to mention a few


Some of our past members are currently pursuing higher studies abroad or are on their peak of careers in different parts of the world, specifically the USA and the UK. Over the years our club has generated a large number of members into The Responsible Youth

Deonar Culture

Rotaract Club of Deonar holds its prestige in its people


A club that stands together in joy and happiness and also shares its atrocities together


Our unique club culture nurtures every individual to grow towards community service, compassion, professional development and maintains high ethical standards. Not only that, our huge alumni base and support from the Rotary Club of Deonar has helped our members achieve the best

DEONAR GENERAL BODY MEETIGNS

Rotaract Club of Deonar is a family. And family discussions are important right?


We meet twice a month to share love, joy and happiness. Sometimes we share sorrow and tensions too, but that's okay! As a part of our unique General Body Meetings, we discuss a Reason to Smile with our members each time we meet


We felicitate members for their good work and also conduct fun activities, outings and club dinners. After all a family that eats together stays together!

CLUB SERVICE

Club Service focuses on strengthening fellowship and ensuring the smooth functioning of Rotaract clubs.
It helps in embracing the culture of the club and ensures micro level coordination of the club members.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Community Service encourages passion for social service, helping the communities around the club grow and helping them sustain peace.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Professional Development helps the members improve their capabilities and skills through training and workshops.
It also encourages members to keep updated on trends, scope and technologies around the world.

INTERNATIONAL SERVICE

International Service exemplifies our global reach in promoting peace and understanding.
We support this service avenue by sponsoring or volunteering on international projects, seeking partners abroad, and more.

SPORTS

An all round development of members has been a key motto of Rotaract.
