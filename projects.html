OUR PROJECTS

Here's the Deonar way of serving community!

XPRESSIONS

To propound a comprehensive view, X-PRESSIONS falling under the Community Service Avenue, is one of the marquee events of RC Deonar. It organises and hosts a 2-day Annual Inter-school Talent Festival in the field of Arts, mainly for the school children comprising of around 50 events together in the field of Performing, Literary and Fine Arts. Presently, entering in it's 30th year, this mega event witnesses a participation of 5000+ school children, both primary and secondary from around 100+ schools . Some of them being St. Gregorios , St. Anthony’s girls high school, OlPS , General Education Academy, RBK international,Ryan International from the eastern suburbs of Mumbai and Navi Mumbai amongst others. X-PRESSIONS is a festival in its own where young talent is given a platform to shine in around 50 events and is keenly awaited by school kids and parents alike!

SPEAK UP

‘Speak Up!’ is an initiative by the club under the ‘Professional Development’ avenue which aims at imparting basic communication and public speaking skills for secondary school students to provide these students with an important skill set which may not be a part of their school curriculum.
The speak up module covers topics like elocutions, debates and group discussions.


The project is divided into TWO phases:
Train the Trainers – Where the volunteers will be trained by a professional trainer on aspects of public speaking for the students of 8th and 9th standard.
Training – Where the students will be trained by the volunteers in basic aspects of public speaking through interactive games and other activities for 4 sessions.

TALENT FIESTA

Talent Fiesta is an event that gives individuals with disabilities a platform to showcase their talents through various forms of performing arts and fine arts.
Over 300 students from various parts of Mumbai participate in the event.
It’s a competition wherein everyone who participates, wins, because we believe that each and every one of the participants is a winner in their own right. Details of the event are given in the attached event letter.


We believe that Talent Fiesta will enable participants to display their creative potential, be it through Performing Arts or Fine Arts.
Some of the reputed institutions that are a part of this project are NASEOH, Rochiram School for Hearing Handicapped, Sevadaan, Visions Bhandup and Borivali to name a few.

ROTARY YOUTH LEADERSHIP AWARDS

RYLA stands for Rotary Youth Leadership Awards that are conducted by the Rotary and the Rotaract Clubs in order to encourage the youth in forums of “Leadership”, “Communication”, “Confidence” and “Self Development”


This platform is meant for pushing the youth out of their comfort zone in order to test themselves, learn and gain as much as possible from opportunities provided.


Deonar RYLA is a two day leadership camp that includes speaker sessions and activities for the youth.
We assure self growth in every participant. Not only is RYLA a life changing experience, it motivates and challenges each individual to the fullest.
